About Me

I am a PhD student passionate about understanding the complex dynamics of infectious diseases in ecological systems. My work bridges ecology, epidemiology, and evolutionary biology to address fundamental questions about disease transmission, host-pathogen coevolution, and the ecological factors that drive disease outbreaks.
